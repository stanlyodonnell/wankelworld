1/24 Nichimo Mazda Savanna RX-7 SA

Street Power 0»»400m Series

Kit No. SP-2405

Click to see instruction manual

 This 1/24 scale Nichimo Mazda Savanna RX-7 is a simple kit to build, but still fairly well detailed. It can be fitted with either a RE-140 or a RE-260 this along with 4 x AA batteries are concealed under the well detailed interior.

The body shell and light covers are molded in black plastic. All glass including headlights are molded in a dark brown tint. While the chassis, interior and steering linkages are in a greyish brown.  The wheel rims, exhaust, rear vision mirror, number plates are molded in a dark cream colour. Tires are Michelin with a fairly chunky tread (this would have been typical of the tyres that where standard issue on the RX-7 of the day). No decals are included in this model kit.

Instruction manual is fairly basic (Japanese text). This kit even comes with gears and battery contacts (for the electric motor) and a little tube of Nichimo glue.
