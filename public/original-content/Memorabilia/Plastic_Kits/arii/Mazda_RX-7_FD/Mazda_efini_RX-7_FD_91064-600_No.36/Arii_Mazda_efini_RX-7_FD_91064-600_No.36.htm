1/32 Arii Midnight FD RX-7

Owners Club Series

Kit No. 91064-600 No. 36

This kit of a 1991 Mazda efini RX-7 is fairly well detailed for its scale. And comes with a nice pair of racing seats, a full roll cage and a nice set of BBS mag wheels included in this kit. The body is molded in white plastic, the windows are in a clear plastic and the rest of the kit is molded in black plastic.
