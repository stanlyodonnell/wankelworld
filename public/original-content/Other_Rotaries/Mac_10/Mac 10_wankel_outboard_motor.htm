MAC 10 Wankel Outboard Motor

       .

Very little information is know about the MAC 10 wankel rotary outboard motor. The MAC Wankel outboard was produced by a Italian company know as Moto Attrezzi Co. (MAC). MAC 10 wankel was joint venture between Fichtel & Sachs and MAC as far as I know they only produced one type of wankel powered outboard motor the Mac 10. 

The wankel engine for the MAC 10 was produced by Fichtel & Sachs, this was there KM48 engine a 160 cm3, single rotor, air cooled engine developing approximately 8hp, so really maybe it should be called the MAC 8 not MAC 10. MAC (Moto Attrezzi Co.) produced the leg, cowl and other parts and any other parts needed to complete the outboard motor.  If you have anymore info on this engine please email me at ask_us@drmaz.com
