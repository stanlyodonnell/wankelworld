RX Evolv Wall Paper

I have included the two most common sizes for desk top wall paper (1024x768 and 800x600)  plus I have modified all these images to suite my new computer (Sony Vaio PCV - W102) this screen is wider then usual (1280x768) some of these images I modified may be a little distorted,but 99% still look excellent. Please select the size that suites your computer set up. To install, click the text (link) of the file size you would like (1280x768, 1024x768 or 800x600). This will take you to a full size picture, then left click on the image and click "save as wallpaper" and the picture will appear as a desktop image. Or, if you click "save picture as" this will save the image on your hard drive in the folder you select. 

Most of these wallpaper images are thanks to the http://www.mazda.co.jp (and other Mazda sites) This is an excellent site well worth a visit.
