1/24 Arii Nikon Mazda 737

Group-C Sprint Car Series

Kit No. AR273-1200 No. 3

 

   This kit of a 1985 Nikon Mazda 737C Le Mans Group C Racer  detail is fairly poor. But this is a pretty interesting model to start with it is a battery powered model with a Mabuchi RE280 electric motor. It also has a very interesting chassis in the instructions they call it a "Special Plasticzation Shassis" (yes they did spell chassis "shassis") the plasticzation looks very similar to resin. The body shell is a clear thin plastic, like the body shells of a lot of remote control cars. All other parts including a bummy race driver figure are all molded in white plastic.

   Another neat trick is that this model has a bump switch ( well that's what I'm calling it).  How this works is when the model is traveling forward and hits a wall or some other obstacle it flick a switch that connected to a front and rear bumper and this send the car in the reverse direction. And the opposite happens when the car hits a wall going in reverse.
