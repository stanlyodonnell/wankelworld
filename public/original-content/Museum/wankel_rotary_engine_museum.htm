Wankel Rotary Engine Museum

I have a large collection of Mazda and Wankel related memorabilia. I am currently planning a large Mazda and Wankel Museum. This museum will be located just south of Brisbane city centre (Australia). In this museum we will have a large collection of mainly wankel rotary powered vehicles, stationary engine, power equipment (Lawn mower, Chain saw etc) plus much much more. In the coming months we will be getting some architectural plan drawn up. And we will be posting these on the site.
