1/24 Aoshima Ita-Sha FC3S RX-7

Kit No.  #04

 

 
 
This 1/24 scale Aoshima "Ita-Sha" series kit # 04. "Itasha" literally means "pain car" in Japanese, suggesting that these cars garishly decorated with colorful anime character graphics are embarrassingly painful to look at, or ride in. A bit harsh, perhaps, but there you have it!

This nice Mazda Savanna RX-7 Late Type kit features graphics from the popular anime "Little Busters." The decals for the graphics are particularly well done, featuring image fidelity not often seen in automotive kits. Aside from the decals, this is Aoshima's standard curbside kit (no engine detail) of the RX-7. The body is molded in white. Includes Advan Model 5 wheels.
