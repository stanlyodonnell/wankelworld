Arii `56 Mazda T2000

Owners Club Series 41018-600 #18

 

This model and all the Arii "Owners Club Series" models are 1/32 scale so naturally they aren't as detailed as some of the larger scale models. But even so the Arii `56 Mazda T2000 Tricycle (three wheeled truck)  has a fairly well detailed undercarriage, I think that this model has more detail then some of the other Arii "Owners Club Series" models. The interior detail is fairly basic, what impressed me about this model is the detail in the rear tray it has wooden planks molded into it and nice little hinges on the drop side.

All the parts to this kit are molded in a light blue plastic, with the exception of the windows and head lights which are of cause clear plastic.
