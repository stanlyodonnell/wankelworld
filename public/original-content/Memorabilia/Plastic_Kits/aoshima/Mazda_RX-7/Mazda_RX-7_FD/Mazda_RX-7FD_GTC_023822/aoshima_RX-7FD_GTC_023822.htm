1:24 Aoshima Mazda RX-7 FD3S GT-C

S Package Ver. R

Kit# 0023822 1800
 

 

 
  This was first released in July of 1999. It is of awesome RX-7 version Mazdaspeed's GT-Concept. The kit is a updated version of Kit #0000114-87 except in this kit the front bumper is molded separately and has the updated circular tail lights and new front light covers. you also get the full Mazdaspeed GT-C Aero Kit with side and rear skirts, front spoiler and high mounted rear spoiler, authentic 18in Mazdaspeed MS-01 Mag wheels painted in gold fitted with a set of Bridgestone 245/40 G-Grid tires (you also get a set of standard Mazda RX-7 FD mags), a new exhaust system (chrome plated), a large oil cooler, Mazdaspeed dash instrument decals (red numbers on white backing) and a new sports steering wheel.

The body and all aero parts are molded in metallic blue, the under carriage, suspension and driveline are all molded in black plastic. While all interior parts are molded in white, with the exception of the new sports steering wheel which is molded in the same metallic blue as the body. All windows and light lens are mold in clear plastic.
