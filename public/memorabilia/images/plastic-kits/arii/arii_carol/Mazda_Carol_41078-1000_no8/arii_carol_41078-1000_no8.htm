Arii `62 Mazda Carol

Owners Diorama Club Series
This is basically Arii kit # 41008-600 with some extras to make a diorama kit of semi rural Japan, the extras you get with this Arii Kit #41078-1000 NO.8 are a telephone pole, a dog house, a large metal drum and a Bamboo fence, and a clothes line, also included in this kit is a plastic base to set your diorama up on.

All the car parts are molded in white plastic with the exception of the windows (this is the same as kit# 41008-600) and all the diorama parts (pole, dog house, drum and bamboo fence) are molded in gray plastic and the base is molded in black plastic.

The "Owners Diorama Club Series" is among of my favorite kits in the Arii range, but hey that's just me.
