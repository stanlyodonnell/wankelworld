Arii `60 Mazda R360 

Owners Club Series

This is the Arii "`60 Mazda R360" from the "Owners Club Series" it is a 1/32 scale model kit #41015-600 NO. 15 and as with all the other models from the "Owners Club Series" it is a fairly basic model with only minimal detail, but this dose make it a very is model to make. That is unless you are like me and add your own detail to models. I try and add as much detail as possible to every model I build, this is just a personal thing (when it comes to anything to do with Mazda's or Rotaries I tend to be a bit of a perfectionist).

When Arii printed this box they made an error, see if you can find it. If you can find it please send me an email contact@drmaz.com.

There is a grand total of 32 parts to this kit, all of witch are molded in white plastic except for the windows and headlights.
