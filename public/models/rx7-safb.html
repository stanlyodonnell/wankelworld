Mazda RX-7 SA/FB

The original. Light, pure, rotary perfection.

Engine12A
Power100-165 hp
Weight~1,000 kg
LayoutFront-mid engine

Overview

The original RX-7. Designed from the ground up as a pure sports car, the SA/FB established the formula: front mid-engine, rear-wheel drive, perfect weight distribution, and that unmistakable rotary sound.

The RX-7 saved Mazda during the oil crisis. Its efficient, compact rotary engine delivered sports car performance without the fuel consumption penalty of larger piston engines.

Legacy

The first-generation RX-7 proved that Mazda could build a world-class sports car. Its success established the RX-7 as an icon and ensured the rotary engine would have a future in performance vehicles.
