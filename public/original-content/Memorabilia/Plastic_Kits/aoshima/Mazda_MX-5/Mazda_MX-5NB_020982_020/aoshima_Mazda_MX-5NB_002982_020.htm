1/24 Aoshima Mazda Roadster NB

The Best Car 1998

Kit No. 020982-1300 #20

  
 

 
   
  This kit is of the curbside model Mazda Roadster GF-NB8C from Aoshima's "The Best Car 1998" series, kit #020982-1300 No.20.

Because this kit is of the curbside model Mazda Roadster NB, there aren't many optional parts included in this kit. However you do get the option of having the convertible top either up or down, I personally like it down but that's just me.

One nice touch that Aoshima has done with all of its Mazda Roadster NB kits is include accelerator, clutch and brake paddles not so many kit have this.

All body and interior parts are molded in white plastic, the chassis and all suspension, exhaust and brake parts are molded in black plastic. The open convertible top is also molded in black plastic, while the close convertible top is molded in clear plastic.
