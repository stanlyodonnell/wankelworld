Alturdyne/Alturair

Alturdyne produces range extenders for hybrid electric vehicles (EVs) using primarily rotary engines. Available sizes are 15-25 kW, 40 kW and 75 kW. Larger sizes can be developed and various voltages can be provided. Rotary engines will operate on gasoline, natural gas or propane. Low sound levels can be provided. Emission levels for nitrogen oxides (NOX) are less than two grams per horsepower hour (g/hp-hr) and can be reduced further using a catalytic converter.

Alturdyne assembles the rotary engine coupled with a variety of generators for lightweight, rugged, reliable and cost-effective power generation. The generator set with electric start rotary engine is exceptionally quiet, vibration free, has low emissions and a long life. It is well suited for use where minimum size and light weight are desirable.

Alturdyne produces  a range of their own design Wankel rotaries as we as using Mazda's 13B and 20B rotary engine in some of their generators.
