1/24 Aoshima Mazda Roadster NB

The Best Car 1998

Kit No. 020982-1300 #20

  
 

 
   
  This kit is of the AutoExe Mazda Roadster NB8C from Aoshima's "S Package Ver. R" series. It is very similar to kit #020982-1300 No.20.

The main differences are the Mazda standard nose cone has been replaced with the AutoExe NB-02 nose cone, a AutoExe rear wing and roll over bar have also been added. The closed soft top from  kit #020982-1300 No.20 has been changed to a Hard top, but the open top is still included, so you can choose the style you like the best, open or closed. The only other changes are the Mazda Standard wheels have been swapped for SSR GP wheels these are prepainted in silver and the inclusion of a AutoExe decal set.

The body and body kit are molded in white plastic. The interior parts are now molded in black plastic instead of white plastic as in  kit #020982-1300 No.20. All chassis, exhaust, suspension and brake parts are molded in black plastic.
