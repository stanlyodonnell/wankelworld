1/20 Doyusha Mazda Cosmo AP (RX-5)

Nostalgic Heroes Series

Kit #NH 12-1200 No.12

 

  
   
 
  This is a 1/20 scale kit from Doyusha of the 1976 Mazda Cosmo AP. It is a standard curb side model. The model it's self is pretty simple, although it dose have an opening bonnet the engine detail is very basic. The interior is also very basic, as is the chassis. I guess they have kept it fairly simple because it is a motorized model ( no excuse if you ask me).

The body is molded in white, all chassis and interior parts are molded in black plastic. While the head light and tail light assembly, front and rear bumpers, grill, wheels, number plates and the side and rear view mirrors are all molded in bright chrome plate. All windows are in a smoky brown colour.
