Mazda RX-3 / Savanna

Racing champion. 7-time Japanese GP winner.

Engine10A/12A
Power100-130 hp
Racing Titles7× JGP
BodyCoupe/Sedan/Wagon

Overview

The RX-3 became Mazda's rotary-powered warrior in touring car racing, dominating the Japanese Grand Prix and establishing the rotary's motorsport credentials.

The RX-3 won the Japanese Grand Prix for seven consecutive years (1972-1978), cementing its place in motorsport history. Its success on track translated to strong sales and passionate fans worldwide.

Racing Heritage

The Savanna RX-3 was virtually unbeatable in Japanese touring car racing. Its 12A engine could be tuned for impressive power, while the lightweight body and balanced handling made it a formidable competitor. The distinctive sound of a racing RX-3 became synonymous with Japanese motorsport in the 1970s.
