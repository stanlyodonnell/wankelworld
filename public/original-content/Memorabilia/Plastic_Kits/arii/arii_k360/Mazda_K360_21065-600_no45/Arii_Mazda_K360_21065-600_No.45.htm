Arii `58 Mazda K360

Owners Diorama Club Series #21065 NO.45

Note that this kit is not listed on this Arii model list.

This is basically Arii kit # 41018-600 #17 with the addition of a roof for the tray back. All the car parts are molded in white plastic with the exception of the windows which is molded in clear plastic (this is the same as kit# 41018-600) and the roof for the tray back which is in a dark green. One interesting note with this kit, is that on the reverse side of the instructions for all the Arii "Owners Diorama Club Series" a list of the model in this series  appears, but Kit No. 21065 #45 is not listed. So it seems that this kit was an after thought.

That been said the "Owners Diorama Club Series" is still among of my favorite kits in the Arii range, but hey that's just me.
