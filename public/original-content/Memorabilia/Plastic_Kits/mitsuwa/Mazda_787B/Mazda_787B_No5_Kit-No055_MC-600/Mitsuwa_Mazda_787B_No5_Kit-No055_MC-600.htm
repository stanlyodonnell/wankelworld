Mitsuwa Charge Mazda 787B #55

RC-1 Kit No. 621 MMR-500

 

Mitsuwa Mazda Charge 787B Le Mans wining car #55. This is a reboxed version of kit RC-1 Kit No. 621 MMR-500. Just like kit RC-1 Kit No. 621 MMR-500 the body of this kit is molded in orange plastic with green triangle details painted on the body. All other parts molded in black with the exception of the wheels which are mate Chrome finish where as with kit # kit RC-1 Kit No. 621 MMR-500 the wheels are molded in white plastic. Both front (the front being slightly small in diameter) and back tyres are molded in black rubber.
