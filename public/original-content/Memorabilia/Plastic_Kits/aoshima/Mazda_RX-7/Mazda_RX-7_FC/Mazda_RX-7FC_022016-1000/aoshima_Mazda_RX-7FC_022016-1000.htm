1/32 Aoshima Initial D FC3S RX-7

Kit No. 022016-1000 #02

This 1/32 scale Aoshima Initial D (Japanese comic) kit # 022016-1000 is a very basic kit which requires no gluing. It has no interior detail at all, in place of the interior, is space for a FA-130 motor and two AA sized batteries (these are not included). To hide the FA-130 motor and batteries this kit comes with a very dark (nearly black) window section.

The body is molded in a off white colour plastic, the rest of the kit is molded in black plastic with the exception of the wheels which are molded in grey and the windows which are dark tinted. This kit includes a gear set for the FA-130 motor and even a small packet of grease!!
