Mazda RX-2 / Capella Rotary

Mid-size rotary sedan

Engine12A
Power120-130 hp
BodyCoupe/Sedan
PlatformCapella

Overview

The RX-2 combined the practicality of a mid-size sedan with the smooth power of the new 12A rotary engine. It featured a larger 1,146cc displacement and brought rotary refinement to a wider audience.

The RX-2 helped establish the "RX" naming convention that would become legendary in Mazda's lineup. Available as both coupe and sedan, it offered families a taste of rotary performance without sacrificing practicality.
