Comotor

The Company Comotor SA was a was born out of the ashes of the company combil which was joint venture between NSU and Citroën. Comotor was started in Luxembourg in April 1967. Its goal was to produce Wankel rotary engines for the Citroën M35 prototype and  the later Comotor 624 engine for the Citroen GS Birotor.

There where many technical difficulties for the company to over everything from gas tightness , high fuel consumption and reliability　issues. Even though Citroën took a calculated development path, the rising fuel prices of which was the result of the oil crisis in 1973  was the final nail in the coffin for the demand Comotor wankel engine. I belive that this was a major contributed to the down fall of Comotor , Citroen and NSU
