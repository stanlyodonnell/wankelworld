Mitsuwa Namco Mazda Savanna RX-7

Deformed Mid Racer

Mazda RX-7 FC Turbo I believe this is a reboxed Mitsuwa A-13 Kit No.153 produced for the Namco Company. This model is in a very unusual triangle box, its the first time I have seen this.

The body of this kit is molded in red plastic with all other parts molded in black with the exception of the wheels which are white plastic. Both front (the front being slightly small in diameter) and back tyres have "PRO MAX RACING" molded into the tyre wall.

I have only ever seen this kit once on a Japanese site so I just had to buy it.
