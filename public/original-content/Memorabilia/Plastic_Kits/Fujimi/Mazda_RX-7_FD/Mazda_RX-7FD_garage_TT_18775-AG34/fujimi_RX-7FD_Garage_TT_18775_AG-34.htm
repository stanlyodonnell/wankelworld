1:24 Fujimi Mazda RX-7 FD3S Garage TT

Race Car

Kit# 18775 AG-34
   
   
This is a new kit from Fujimi, it is very similar to the "Mazda FD3S RX-7 Type RS" Kit #04046, minus the rear wing. The rear wing is still included in this "Garage TT" kit, so you could still add it to the model if you wish.

Other differences are the "Garage TT" kit has a set of 17in Hart CP-035 mag wheels and a set of Pirelli P-Zero tires instead of the Mazda factory wheels. A set of metal shims have been included to give the car the appearance of a wider track like is shown in the picture on the box top. This kit also has an awesome set of decals to recreate the look of the original car.

The body and most interior parts are molded in white plastic while the chassis, suspension, and exhaust parts are molded in the typical black plastic. The wheels have been prepainted in gold, I'm unsure why they chose gold as the wheels on the actual car are silver.
