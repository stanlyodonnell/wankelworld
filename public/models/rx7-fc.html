Engine13B Turbo II

0-606.1 sec

Overview

The second-generation RX-7 brought modern styling, turbocharging, and refined handling. It competed directly with Porsche's 944 and 924, offering similar performance at a lower price point.

The FC introduced the convertible variant and established the RX-7 as a serious performance car on the world stage. Its Turbo II model with intercooler delivered genuine sports car performance.
