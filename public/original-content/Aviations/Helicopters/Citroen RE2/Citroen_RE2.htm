Citroёn RE2

 

Citroёn in the early 1970s, investigated the possibility of producing helicopters with the Wankel engines manufactured by its subsidiary Comotor. Some models, like the Citroën RE2, were flight-tested and still exist. It has the same wankel comotor 622 that was fitted to the Citroёn GS Birotor, with one small difference the motor in the RE2 is mounted vertically and the motor had a dual firing system.
