Mazda's more unusual rotaries

 

 
Above is the Rx 500  it was a concept car that Mazda unveiled at the 197 Tokyo Motor Show. The Rx 500 had a mid mounted 12A rotary engine.  Unfortunately this awesome car didn't make it to production.
This the Mazda Parkway Mini bus it was fitted with a 13B rotary and as far as I know, it was only sold in Japan. I don't know about anyone else but I would love on of these buses. Click Here or the link at the bottom of the page for more info on the Mazda Parkway Rotary Bus.

 

 

 
The Mazda Road Pacer was a cross between a GMH HJ  Kingswood and the Mazda 13B rotary. It was produced from 1975 - 1978   (I think). Click Here  or the link at the bottom of the page for more info on the Mazda Road Pacer.

 

Mazda Mx 03 was unveiled at the 26th Tokyo Motor Show  in  1984. It was powered by a 3 rotor engine. Mazda produced and unveiled at the 1995 Tokyo Motor Show this concept car named the RX 01 to replace the almighty RX-7. The Rx 01 was the predecessor to the RX Evolv. 

This is the Mazda RX-9(Rotary Luce in the Japanese market). The RX-9 never made it to the overseas market. Click Here or the link at the bottom of the page for more info on the RX-9
