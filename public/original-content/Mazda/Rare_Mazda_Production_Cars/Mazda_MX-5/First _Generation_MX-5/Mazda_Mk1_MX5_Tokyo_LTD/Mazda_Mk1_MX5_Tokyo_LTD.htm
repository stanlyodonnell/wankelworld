Mazda MX-5 Tokyo LTD

 

 

One Of The Rarest MX5s Ever Made. The Tokyo Limited was released in the Tokyo market in November 1993. These cars were besed on the S-Package and had M2-1002 interiors in them (except they retained their electric windows and centre console). The interiors may have been 'donated' from unsold M2-1002 cars that sold poorly. And this particular example has an automatic gearbox. This car was offered for sale on a UK web site in February 2013 for £4995. If you have any further information on the Mazda Mk1 MX-5 Tokyo LTD please contact me on the details below.
