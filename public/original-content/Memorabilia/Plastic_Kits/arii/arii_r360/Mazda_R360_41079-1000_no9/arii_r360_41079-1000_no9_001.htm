Arii `60 Mazda R360

1/32 Owners Diorama Series 41079-1000 #9

This is basically Arii kit # 41015-600 #15 with some extras to make a diorama kit of semi rural Japan, the extras you get with this Arii Kit #41079-1000 are a telephone pole, a small stature,  a water well and a wooden fence, a postbox also included in this kit is a plastic base to set your diorama up on.

All the car parts are molded in white plastic with the exception of the windows (this is the same as kit# 41015-600 #15) and all the diorama parts (pole, stachore,  water well and wooden fence) the base is molded in white plastic.

The "Owners Diorama Club Series" is among of my favourite kits in the Arii range, but hey that's just me.
