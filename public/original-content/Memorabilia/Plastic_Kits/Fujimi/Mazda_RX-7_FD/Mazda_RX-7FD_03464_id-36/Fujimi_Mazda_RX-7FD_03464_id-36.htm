1/24 Fujimi Mazda RX-7 FD3S Type RS

"Inch Up" series

Kit #03464 ID-36

 

This kit is of the curbside model Mazda RX-7 FD3SType RS from Fujimi's "Inch Up" series, kit #03464 ID-36.

All body, Body kit and most interior parts are molded in blus plastic, the chassis and all suspension, exhaust and brake parts are molded in black plastic. All windows and lights are molded in clear plastic. While the wheels are matte chrome finish. This kits also the option of three different steering wheels.
