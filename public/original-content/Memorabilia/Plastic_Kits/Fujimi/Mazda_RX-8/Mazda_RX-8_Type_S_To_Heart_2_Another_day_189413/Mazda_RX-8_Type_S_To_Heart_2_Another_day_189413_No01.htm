1:24 Fujimi Mazda RX-8 To Heart 2 Another Day

Ita-Sha Series

Kit# 189413 #01
     
   
     
This is a nice Mazda RX-8 kit features graphics 8from the popular Japanese anime "To Heart 2 Another Day" The decals are very detail and well done. Aside from the decals, this is Fujimi's standard curbside kit (no engine detail) of the RX-8.

"Itasha" literally means "pain car" in Japanese, suggesting that these cars garishly decorated with colorful anime character graphics are embarrassingly painful to look at or ride in. A bit harsh, perhaps, but there you have it!

The body and most interior parts are molded in white plastic while the chassis, suspension, and exhaust parts are molded in the typical black plastic.
