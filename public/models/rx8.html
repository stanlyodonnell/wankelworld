Mazda RX-8

4-door, 4-seat, high-revving Renesis. The final rotary.

EngineRenesis 13B
Power189-238 hp
Redline9,000 RPM
Layout4-door, 4-seat

Overview

The RX-8 redefined the rotary sports car with its unique 4-door, 4-seat configuration and the high-revving Renesis engine. No other car combined sports car handling with genuine practicality.

With its innovative rear-hinged "freestyle" doors, the RX-8 offered easy access to the back seats while maintaining the sleek profile of a sports coupe. The Renesis engine was a significant evolution, featuring side exhaust ports for improved emissions and fuel economy.

The Final Rotary

The RX-8 was the last production rotary-powered car from Mazda. Its discontinuation in 2012 marked the end of an era, but the RX-8 remains beloved for its unique character and driving experience. The Spirit R special edition is particularly sought after by collectors.
