1:24 Aoshima Mazda RX-7 FD3S

Veilside Combat II

Kit No. 029022 #70
  This is the updated version of Aoshima kit No. 025758 #64 not a lot has changed with this kit though (mostly just little details), a new front nose cone, new side mirrors, a new higher rear wing (you do still have the option if the standard wing), the rear tail light section has also been updated to round tail lights, new steering wheel and a new set of decals. The body and associated parts are now molded in white plastic instead of silver. The price has also increased from ¥1800 to ¥2000. But everything else in still the same as Aoshima kit No. 025758 #64.
