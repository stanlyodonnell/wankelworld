Mazda MX-5 M2-1007

 

 
The Model M2-1007 was a 4-door sedan version, , this was envisioned to capture the post-MX-5/Miata Market. There was only (1) full sized car produced, and another 1/5 scale version that was produced as a model for review by Mazda.
