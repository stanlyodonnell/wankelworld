Mitsuwa Mazda Savanna RX-7 Mid Racer

Deformed Mid Racer

This is the newer version of kit #143 A-5, from the "sports Car Series". Mazda RX 7 FC B Fried Chicken Dinner Mid Racer. This kit is very basic and has a fiction motor. Looks very nice when build.

The chassis of this kit is molded in dark grey plastic with all other parts molded in black with the exception of the wheels which are white plastic.
