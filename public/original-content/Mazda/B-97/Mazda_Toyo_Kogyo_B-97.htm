Mazda (Toyo Kogyo) B-97

This was one of the prototypes that never made it to production, but spun other models. There were 30 test models that went through test that later were converted to tri-wheel trucks that carried up to 2tons of cargo. Toyo Kogyo soon became the leader of mini-utility vehicles in Japan.
