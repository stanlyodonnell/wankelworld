Forums & Online Communities

The rotary community thrives online. These forums are invaluable resources for technical advice, troubleshooting, and connecting with fellow enthusiasts.

RX-7 Club

One of the largest RX-7 communities online. Covers all generations with active technical sections, build threads, and marketplace.

  • www.rx7club.com

Rotary Forums

General rotary discussion covering all Mazda rotary models. Great for technical deep-dives and engine building advice.

  • www.rotaryforums.com

Australia/New Zealand

  • Australian RX-7 Forum: Strong community for Australian owners
  • SAU (Skyline Australia): Also has active rotary sections

Facebook Groups

Many active Facebook groups exist for specific models and regions. Search for "RX-7," "RX-8," or "rotary" plus your location to find local communities.

Parts Suppliers

Keeping a rotary running requires access to quality parts. These suppliers specialize in rotary components.

United States

  • Atkins Rotary: Comprehensive catalog of rotary parts
  • Racing Beat: Performance parts and accessories
  • Rx-7 Specialties: New and used parts
  • Mazdatrix: Racing and performance specialists

United Kingdom

  • Rotary Revs: UK-based rotary specialists
  • Rotary Motion: Parts and servicing

Australia

  • Rotary Performance Australia: Performance parts and engines
  • Mazda Motorsports Australia: Factory performance parts

Japan

  • RE Amemiya: Legendary rotary tuning house
  • Pan Speed: High-performance rotary specialists
  • Feed: Body kits and performance parts
  • Panspeed: Racing and street performance

Rotary Specialists

When you need expert help with your rotary, these shops have the knowledge and experience to keep your engine healthy.

Engine Builders

Rotary engines require specific expertise. These builders specialize in rotary engine assembly:

  • Defined Autoworks (USA): High-performance rotary builds
  • Banzai Racing (USA): FD RX-7 specialists
  • Pettit Racing (USA): Turbo and naturally aspirated builds
  • Rotary Performance (AUS): Australian rotary experts

General Service

For maintenance, diagnosis, and general repairs, find a shop that knows rotaries:

  • Search forums for recommended shops in your area
  • Ask local car clubs for referrals
  • Mazda dealerships may have rotary-trained technicians

Clubs & Organizations

Join the rotary community in person. Clubs organize meets, track days, and social events.

International

  • International RX-8 Club: Global RX-8 community
  • Rotary Aviation Association: For aviation rotary enthusiasts

United States

  • SevenStock: Annual mega-meet in California
  • Regional RX-7 Clubs: Most major cities have active clubs

Australia

  • Rotary Club of Australia: National rotary enthusiast organization
  • State-based clubs: QLD, NSW, VIC, WA all have active scenes

How to Find Your Local Scene

  • Check Facebook for local rotary groups
  • Attend Cars & Coffee events—rotary owners often gather
  • Ask at local Mazda specialists
  • Check track day schedules—rotary owners love the track

Media & Content

Books, videos, and podcasts for rotary enthusiasts.

YouTube Channels

  • Rob Dahm: Rotary builds and education
  • Haltech: Technical rotary content
  • Various build channels: Search for "RX-7 build" or "rotary rebuild"

Podcasts

  • Search automotive podcasts for rotary-specific episodes
  • Many general car podcasts feature rotary content

Magazines

  • Japanese Nostalgic Car: Often features classic rotaries
  • Super Street: Modified rotary content
  • Rotary-specific publications: Check forums for recommendations

Heritage & Museums

Experience rotary history in person.

Museums

  • Mazda Museum (Hiroshima, Japan): The ultimate rotary pilgrimage. Features historic rotary cars including Le Mans winners.
  • Mazda Classic Automobil Museum (Augsburg, Germany): European Mazda history
  • Various automotive museums: Many have rotary cars in their collections

Historic Events

  • Le Mans Classic (France): Historic racing where 787Bs sometimes appear
  • Goodwood Festival of Speed (UK): Often features rotary-powered cars
  • Monterey Motorsports Reunion (USA): Historic racing event

Mazda Heritage Collection

Mazda maintains a heritage collection of significant vehicles. While not always open to the public, they occasionally display cars at events and exhibitions.
