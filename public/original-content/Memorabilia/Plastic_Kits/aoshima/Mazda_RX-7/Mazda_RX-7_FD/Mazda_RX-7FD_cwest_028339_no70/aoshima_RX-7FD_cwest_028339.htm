1:24 Aoshima Mazda RX-7 FD3S C-West

S Package Ver. R Series

Kit No. 028339 #70
This kit is yet another work of art with lots of detail by Aoshima, and from what I could find out about the actual C-West RX-7  it is pretty accurately detailed. But unlike most other Aoshima kits it doesn't give you many options for personalizing. With this kit the only option you get to personalize are the wheels, you get to choose between the standard Mazda RX-7 FD3S wheels or the ADVAN RG Mag wheels.

The body (including the body kit) and interior are all molded in white plastic, the chassis and under body parts are molded in black plastic, the standard RX-7 wheels are matt silver, and the ADVAN RG wheels are a matt gold colour.
