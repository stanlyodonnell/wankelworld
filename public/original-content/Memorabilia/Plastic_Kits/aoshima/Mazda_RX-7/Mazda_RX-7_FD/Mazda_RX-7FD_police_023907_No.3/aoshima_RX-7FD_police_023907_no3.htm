1/24 Aoshima RX-7 FD

The Patrol Car

Kit No. 0023907-1600 No.3

  
 

 
   
  This is a basic curbside Mazda RX-7 with the addition of some police related accessories. These accessories include police lights, siren, radio, witches hats (safety cones), badges and some sort of wind deflector for the bonnet (if you know what this is used for please email me contact@drmaz.com ). In the decal set you get a big list of Japanese Prefecture names written in kanji (Chinese looking characters) you simply choose the one you like the best and put it on the side of the car. There is also some big number for the roof of the car. And to make painting a little easier Aoshima has included a stencil for the black around the bottom of the car.

The body, body parts and interior are all molded in white plastic. All the suspension, exhaust, brake and Chassis parts are molded in black plastic. While all police related accessories, glass and lights are molded in clear plastic. The standard Mazda RX-7 FD3S wheels are plated in satin chrome.
