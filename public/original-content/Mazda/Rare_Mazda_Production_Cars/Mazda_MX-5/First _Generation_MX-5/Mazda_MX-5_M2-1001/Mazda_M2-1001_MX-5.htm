Mazda MX-5 M2-1001

 

 
M2 was Mazda's relatively shortlived department for concept cars that were built in very limited numbers. The M2-1001 is highly sought after, even in Japan where mint examples can fetch well over £10,000.

 
Years Produced:
1991 (December)
No. Produced:
300
Colours:
Brave Blue
Spec:
1.6 Engine Only (Special ECU with 130ps)
Garage Hundred One Nosecone
Rear Lip Boot Spoiler
Rear Upper Mudflap Trim
Aluminium Roll Bar
Aluminium Door Mirrors
Aluminium Fuel Filler Cap (with seperate key to main key)
Aluminium Gear Knob
Aluminium Window Winders
Aluminium Pedals and Foot Rest
Front Engine Strut Brace (Aluminium)
M2 Serial Number Plate on Passenger Door and Engine Bay
M2 Keys
M2 Leather Drivers' Side Doorbag
Bucket Seats
Mechanical Cable LSD (Limited Slip Differential)
