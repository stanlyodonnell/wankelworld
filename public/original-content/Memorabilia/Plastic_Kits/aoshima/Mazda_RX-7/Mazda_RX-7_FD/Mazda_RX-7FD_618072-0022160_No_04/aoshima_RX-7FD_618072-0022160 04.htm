1/32 Aoshima Initial D Mazda RX-7 FD3S

Kit No. 0022160 04

    This 1/32 scale Aoshima Initial D (Japanese comic) kit # 00220160-1000 is a very basic kit of a Mazda RX-7 FD3S, which requires no gluing. It has no interior detail at all, in place of the interior, is space for a FA-130 motor and two AA sized batteries (these are not included). To hide the FA-130 motor and batteries this kit comes with a very dark (nearly black) window section.

The body is molded in a off white colour plastic, the rest of the kit is molded in black plastic with the exception of the wheels which are molded in grey. This kit includes a gear set for the FA-130 motor and even a small packet of grease!!
