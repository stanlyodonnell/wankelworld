1/24 Fujimi Mazda RX-7 SA Turbo

Kit No. ??? ID-4

  
 

 
  This kit is very similar to Fujimi Kit #00307 SM-7 Mazda Savanna RX-7 Racing, except that this kit dosn't have the racing decals. The decals that are included in this kit suggest that the car is a Japanese spec 1984 RX-7 SA with a 12A Turbo. The 12A Turbo was only produce for the Japanese market. The only other difference that I can see is the mags have been changed to Impul (?) mags (I'm not 100% sure on these mags as I was just a pretty young when these wheels where kickin' around)
