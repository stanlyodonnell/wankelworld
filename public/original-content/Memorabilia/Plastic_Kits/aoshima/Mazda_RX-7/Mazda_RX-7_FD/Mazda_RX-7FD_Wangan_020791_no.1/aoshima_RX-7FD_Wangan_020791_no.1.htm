The kit its self is a nice looking kit with a choice of 2 different type of wheels, the Speedline Competition wheels shown on top of the box or the standard έfini RX-7 FD3S wheels. You also have a choice  between the a Huge high mounted rear wing (shown on the box) or the standard έfini RX-7 FD3S rear wing. Included in this kit is 2 Supertrapp mufflers  and a nice set of decals. In the set of decals there are  2 types of racing harnesses for the seats. One thing I find very strange about this decal set is they have included Nissan, Nismo, Toyota and TRD decals, what for I do not know (perhaps this is a standard set of race decals that are used for a lot of different cars or some thing like that).

The body, body kit and all interior parts are molded in a deep yellow colour, the chassis, suspension, exhaust and brakes are molded in black plastic. The wheels and Supertrapp muffler are painted in silver. The tires are G Grid.
