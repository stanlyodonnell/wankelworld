1/28 Blue tank Mazda Savanna RX-7

Super Car Series

Kit #TK-08822

 

  
   
   
This is a 1/28 scale kit from Blue Tank. It was originally made by Nitto, kit #14056 No.3 and Blue Tank reboxed this kit. The model it's self is pretty simple, consisting of a one-piece body shell (e.g. no opening bonnet or doors), a low detailed interior and a fairly simple floor pan.

The front wheels steer, not very well but they do work. There is an electric motor is hidden at the rear of the car, under the rear shelf and a single AA-size battery is hidden under the bonnet. So a fairly simple kit, but still worthy of inclusion in your collection.
