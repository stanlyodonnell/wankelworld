Mazda RX-4 / Luce Rotary

Flagship luxury rotary

Engine12A/13A
Power125-135 hp
BodyCoupe/Sedan/Wagon
LuxuryExecutive

Overview

Mazda's flagship rotary sedan combined luxury appointments with smooth rotary power, competing in the executive car segment. The RX-4, also known as the Luce Rotary, was Mazda's attempt to prove the rotary engine could power a premium vehicle.

Available as a 2-door coupe, 4-door sedan, and even a station wagon, the RX-4 offered the space and comfort expected of a luxury car with the unique character of the 13A engine.
